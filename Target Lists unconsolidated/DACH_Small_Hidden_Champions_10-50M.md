## NOTES ON METHODOLOGY

### Revenue Verification
- Companies marked "est." have estimated revenues based on employee count, industry benchmarks, and available partial data
- The DDW Lexikon der Weltmarktfuehrer (1,469 companies) contains detailed profiles but requires paid access
- The WirtschaftsWoche Weltmarktfuehrerindex classifies "Future Champions" as companies with EUR 5-50M revenue and >40% export share - this is the closest match to your ICP

### Companies EXCLUDED (already in your list or too large)
- All 58 companies from your existing list
- Companies with >EUR 100M revenue (Wemhoener 190M, Koepfer 145M, Gruner 115M global, Faulhaber 253M, Leuze 323M, Baumer 500M, Kistler 448M CHF, Micro-Epsilon 140M, etc.)
- Companies with >500 employees globally where revenue clearly exceeds 50M
