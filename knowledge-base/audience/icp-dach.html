Section 11
DACH Market Notes
Mittelstand

Backbone of the German economy. EUR 50M–500M revenue, 200–2,000 employees. Growth ambitions but limited scaling resources. Ideal segment.

Hidden Champions

World market leaders in niche segments. Unique products (fits exclusivity principle), international ambitions. Perfect ICP match.

Consensus Culture

German decision-making is consensus-driven. Expect 3–5 stakeholders minimum. Budget decisions require Geschäftsführung approval.

Datenschutz

Not just compliance — it's cultural. German buyers feel personally responsible for data decisions. GDPR-native architecture removes their biggest fear.
