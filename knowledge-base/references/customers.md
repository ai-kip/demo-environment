## Reference Usage Rules

1. **Always be specific:** Not "our customers see improvements" but "Aquablu: 60% more meetings, contract the same day"
2. **Show diverse use cases:** Not only sales (Aquablu), also purchasing (iBOOD) and partner models (Acer)
3. **Name numbers:** 300-500% uplift, 10->16 meetings, 33M companies
4. **Use recognizable brands:** Acer for enterprise credibility
5. **Tell stories:** The salesperson who saw the buying signal and signed the contract the same day
